Get an encrypted email buddy

More people than ever want to communicate privately, without creeps collecting their info. Encrypting your email is part of the solution. It's not hard, learn how→

But not everyone has someone to share encrypted email with. Find a friendly cryptopal right here.

Discover someone who interests you from our list, get their email and public key (what's that?) by clicking on their Key ID, and send a greeting. Or offer yourself as a buddy to meet others encrypting.

Why?

To practice critical skills for private data. To regain the romance of sacred communications and meet new people who care about online privacy.
